About Me

I am currently a HDMap researcher and developer at MoonX Autonomous Driving Company in Shenzhen, China. My research interests are in 3D Reconstruction, Image Processing, SLAM and Deep Learning. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Vision and Computer Graphics from State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang Univeristy .
