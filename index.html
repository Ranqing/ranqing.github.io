About Me

I am currently a HDMap researcher and developer at an autonomous driving start-up company MoonX in Shenzhen, China. My research interests are in Image-based 3D Reconstruction, Image Processing and SLAM. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Vision and Computer Graphics from State Key Lab of CAD&CG, Zhejiang Univeristy and my Bachelor degree in Computer Science from Central China Normal University. During my Ph.D period, I was a Computer Vision Research Intern for autonomous driving at DiDi Research from Jul 2016 to May 2017, working with Dr.Qingxiong Yang. After that, I also did another internship in WeRider.ai for several months, working on sensors calibration algorithms.
